About Us

Our Story

As a young child growing up in my parents' restaurant, I witnessed the power of food to light up faces and unite people—a lesson deeply ingrained in me as a first-generation immigrant. With Momo Korean Eggdrop, we strive to rekindle that same magic, bringing people together through the simple joy of sharing great food.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to craft sandwiches that are more than just delicious—they're made with joy, love, and care. Using fresh, daily-made milk bread, cage-free eggs, and the finest ingredients, we create sandwiches that we hope will spark meaningful connections and moments worth savoring.
